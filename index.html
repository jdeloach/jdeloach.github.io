Research

I am broadly interested in machine learning and how it can leverage big data to learn new knowledge. Much of my recent work in this field has focused on the applications for mobile app vetting and working with large (~9 TB) datasets with questionable ground truth data. With one paper down, we are now beginning to investigate if and how semi-supervised learning could be applicable in the app vetting space.

I am a research assistant in the Machine Learning and Bioinformatics Laboratory at Kansas State University and am advised by Dr. Doina Caragea, and colloborate frequently with Dr. Simon Ou.
